How to use your PreBlock API-Key

Full detailed guide is on the way. This page gets you productive in 60 seconds — everything you need to test your key against the live endpoint.

1 · Base URL

All requests go to:

https://api.preblock.io

You authenticate by sending your API-key as the api_key query-parameter. There is no OAuth / bearer-token flow — a single long-lived key per license.

2 · Health check (no key needed)

Confirm the endpoint is reachable from your side:

curl https://api.preblock.io/api/health

Expected: {"status":"ok",...}

3 · Your first call

🎁 Community tier

You can call /api/templates/preview to see how PreBlock would score a hypothetical block reward. Read-only, 30 requests / day.

curl "https://api.preblock.io/api/templates/preview?api_key=YOUR_KEY&fees_sats=15000000"

The full scored template endpoint (/scored/next) is Starter-only — this is where you upgrade from testing to real mining.

₿ Starter tier

You get both endpoints. /scored/next returns a fully optimized template ready to hand to your mining pipeline.

curl "https://api.preblock.io/api/templates/scored/next?api_key=YOUR_KEY"

Rate limit: 100 requests / minute. That is enough to refresh templates once per second and still leave headroom for retries.

4 · Rate-limit response

If you exceed your tier's limit, the response is HTTP 429 with a Retry-After header in seconds. Client libraries should respect this — don't hammer.

5 · Store your key safely

Your key is delivered once, in the welcome email. On our side we only store the bcrypt hash — if you lose the plaintext key, we cannot recover it and have to issue a new one (which invalidates the old).

Best practice: paste the key straight from the welcome email into a password manager (1Password, Bitwarden, KeePassXC).

6 · Deeper integration paths

The API-key gives you the fastest path. Once you are comfortable, we support three deeper integration patterns:

  • Getblocktemplate override: drop-in replacement for your Bitcoin Core / Knots node's getblocktemplate RPC — no code changes on your mining side, just a config-file edit.
  • DATUM adapter (OCEAN Pool): feed the optimized templates directly into the DATUM protocol.
  • SV2 Job-Declarator: for Braiins Pool and modern Stratum-v2 pools.
